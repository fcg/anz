ANZSCO 3 技术员和技工 MAJOR GROUP 3 TECHNICIANS AND TRADES WORKERS - FLYabroad

ANZSCO 3 技术员和技工职责概述 - FLYabroad

技术员和技工应用广泛的或深入的技术、技工或行业特定的知识，执行一系列的技术工作，经常支持科学、工程、建筑和制造业活动。TECHNICIANS AND TRADES WORKERS perform a variety of skilled tasks, applying broad or in-depth technical, trade or industry specific knowledge, often in support of scientific, engineering, building and manufacturing activities.

ANZSCO 3 技术员和技工技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

这一类别的大多数职业对学历和工作经验的要求如下：Most occupations in this major group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below.

在澳大利亚： In Australia:

  • AQF大专学历、高级文凭或毕业证书，或至少3年的相关工作经验（ANZSCO 2级水平）；AQF Associate Degree, Advanced Diploma or Diploma, or at least three years of experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 2); or
  • AQF3级证书，包括2年在职培训经验，或者AQF4级证书，或至少3年的相关工作经验（ANZSCO 3级水平）。AQF Certificate III including at least two years of on-the-job training, or AQF Certificate IV or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)

新西兰： In New Zealand:

  • 新西兰注册文凭，或3年以上的相关工作经验（ANZSCO 2级水平）；或 NZ Register Diploma, or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 2); or
  • 新西兰4级注册文凭，或3年以上的相关工作经验（ANZSCO 3级水平）。NZ Register Level 4 qualification, or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)

有些情况下，除了正式的学历要求，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训经历。 In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification.

ANZSCO 3 技术员和技工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

  • 执行测试和实验，并为健康专业人员、自然和物理科学专业人员和工程技术人员提供技术支持。 carrying out tests and experiments, and providing technical support to Health Professionals, Natural and Physical Science Professionals and Engineering Professionals
  • 为计算机软硬件用户提供技术支持。 providing technical support to users of computer hardware and software
  • 装配、修理和保养铁质、木制、玻璃和纺织产品。 fabricating, repairing and maintaining metal, wood, glass and textile products
  • 修理和保养机动车辆、飞机、船舶以及电气、电子产品和设备。repairing and maintaining motor vehicles, aircraft, marine craft and electrical and electronic machines and equipment
  • 构造、修理、装修建筑物等。constructing, repairing, fitting-out and finishing buildings and other structures
  • 操作印刷和装订设备。 operating printing and binding equipment
  • 准备并烹饪食物。 preparing and cooking food
  • 为动物剪毛，照顾、培训、喂养动物，并协助兽医。shearing, caring for, training and grooming animals, and assisting Veterinarians
  • 繁殖及栽培植物，为体育赛事铺设和维护草皮。 propagating and cultivating plants, and establishing and maintaining turf surfaces for sporting events
  • 理发、造型。 cutting and styling hair
  • 操作化学、天然气、石油和电力设备。 operating chemical, gas, petroleum and power generation equipment
  • 为文艺演出的制片、录音和广播提供技术帮助。 providing technical assistance for the production, recording and broadcasting of artistic performances
