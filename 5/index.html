ANZSCO 5 文书和行政人员 MAJOR GROUP 5 CLERICAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE WORKERS - FLYabroad

ANZSCO 5 文书和行政人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

文书和行政人员协助经理、专业人员、组织机构来组织、存储、检索信息。 CLERICAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE WORKERS provide support to Managers, Professionals and organisations by organising, storing, manipulating and retrieving information.

ANZSCO 5 文书和行政人员技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

这一类别的大多数职业对学历和工作经验的要求如下： Most occupations in this major group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below.

在澳大利亚： In Australia:

  • AQF大专文凭、高级文凭或证书，或3年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 2级水平）； AQF Associate Degree, Advanced Diploma or Diploma, or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 2); or
  • 或AQF三级证书，包含两年的在职培训经历，或者AQF四级证书，3年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 3级水平）； AQF Certificate III including at least two years of on-the-job training, or AQF Certificate IV, or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 3); or
  • 或AQF二级或三级证书，或1年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 4级水平）； AQF Certificate II or III, or at least one year of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 4); or
  • 或AQF一级证书，或中等义务教育（ANZSCO 5级水平）。AQF Certificate I, or compulsory secondary education (ANZSCO Skill Level 5)

在新西兰 ： In New Zealand:

  • NZ注册文凭，或3年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 2级水平）； NZ Register Diploma, or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 2); or
  • 或NZ4级注册资格，或3年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 3级水平）； NZ Register Level 4 qualification, or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 3); or
  • 或NZ二级或三级注册资格，或1年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 4级水平）； NZ Register Level 2 or 3 qualification, or at least one year of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 4); or
  • 或NZ一级注册资格，或中等义务教育（ANZSCO 5级水平）。 NZ Register Level 1 qualification, or compulsory secondary education (ANZSCO Skill Level 5)

某些情况下，除了正式的学历要求，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训经历。对于技能水平为5级的职业，还需要一个短期的培训，可能是附加的也可能代替正规资格要求，或者不需要正规的资格和在职培训经验。 In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification. In the case of some Skill Level 5 occupations, a short period of on-the-job training may be required in addition to or instead of the formal qualification, or no formal qualification or on-the-job training may be required.

ANZSCO 5 文书和行政人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

  • 管理合同、计划和项目。 administering contracts, programs and projects
  • 制定、审查和控制办公室的职能。 setting, reviewing and controlling office functions
  • 履行文书、秘书、组织者和其他行政职能performing clerical, secretarial, organisational and other administrative functions
  • 处理、编辑文本和数据。 entering, processing and editing text and data
  • 接待客户和访问者，并提供信息帮助。 greeting clients and visitors, and responding to inquiries and requests for information
  • 生产、记录和评估财务、产品、库存和统计信息。 producing, recording and evaluating financial, production, stock and statistical information
  • 接受、处理并发送邮件、文档和资料。receiving, processing and sending mail, documents and information
